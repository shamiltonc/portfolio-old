I use mechanical pencils for sketching (I like colored 0.5mm lead the most). For line work, I use a mix of ballpoint pen and a variety of Sakura Pigma Micron pens. I either color digitally or I use watercolor (paint & pencils). I sometimes also use regular colored pencils or artist markers.
