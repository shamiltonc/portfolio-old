About Me

Hello!

        My name is Samantha Hamilton and I work at Amazon as an IT Support Engineer. In my free time, I dabble in more creative pursuits ranging from web development to art and painting tabletop miniatures.


        The purpose of this website is to showcase some of my works of the coding and creative nature.


        I live in eastern Missouri with my dog Lily. I am a Computer Science graduate (B.S 2020, Southern New Hampshire University) and I also have an Associate in Game Design & Development (A.A.S 2017, Camden County College). I take a lot of inspiration from nature and my favorite video games. My favorite way to spend the day is with a cup of tea, a good book, a sketchbook, and my laptop.
